ABOUT US

Closing the gap between consultant and client

Proteus resourcing was created with the idea of producing a solution for clients that they feel has gone above and beyond what they have previously received. One of the key aspects of recruitment that can distance consultant from client is the faceless aspect, that is why we decided to create a system that shortened this gap as much as possible, consequently we always offer to meet current and prospective clients to discuss any projects or vacancies they may have up and coming. 

Catering to all

Whether you are looking for a contract or permanent resource, our consultants have experience spanning over 20 years specifically dealing with either permanent or contract requirements. That way you will be dealing with someone who understands the information needed to make an excellent job of finding you the right person.

Going the extra mile

Our tried and tested processes are our key to delivering a successful service, consultants are trained thoroughly on these processes so that any part of the recruitment life cycle is completed to a high standard. We are extremely proud of what we have created and aim to be the business that goes the extra mile. We offer an honest and transparent service and believe business should be operated honestly and fairly and that is how we have worked successfully with companies large and small throughout the UK.
